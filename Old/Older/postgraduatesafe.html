Analysis Research Group

We welcome enquiries from potential research students and visitors who wish to work with our Analysis Group. Most of our British and European Union PhD students are wholly or partially funded through EPSRC Doctoral Training Grants. Outstanding graduates of Scottish universities are eligible for the prestigeous Carnegie Trust. Possible sources of funding for the overseas students not holding grants from their home country are:

  • ORSAS scholarship, combined with a University Scholarship

  • Commonwealth Scholarship and Fellowship Plan

  • British Chevening Scholarships

  • British Marshall Scholarships
Applications to all the above grants are made through the school, after the candidate has been offered a place. The group currently has no access to funding for Masters degrees. However, all suitably qualified postgraduate students, including the M.Sc. students, are offered the opportunity of supplementing their income by undergraduate tutoring.

For further information about postgraduate study in Pure Mathematics at the University of St Andrews, contact Dr Lars Olsen, Mathematical Institute, University of St Andrews, St Andrews, Fife, KY16 9SS, Scotland.
