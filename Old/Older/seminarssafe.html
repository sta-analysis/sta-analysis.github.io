Analysis Research Group

Programme for Academic Year 2007-08

A series of lectures for postgraduates, final year undergraduates and anyone else who is interested, to provide background in Fractal Analysis, Multifractal Geometry and Kleinian Kroups, will take place on Tuesday afternoons at 15:00 in Room 3B.

  • Martinmas Semester (Autumn / Winter 2007)

  • Candlemas Semester (Spring 2008)

Candlemas Semester (Spring 2008):


  • Tuesday 26th February 2008

    Lars Olsen: Topological dimensions of Polish spaces and multifractals.

  • Tuesday 13th & Tuesday 20th February 2008

    James Mitchell: Generating dense subgroups of automorphism groups, abstract .

Martinmas Semester (Autumn / Winter 2007):


  • Tuesday 4th December 2007

    Kenneth Falconer: Glass filling, Minkowski measurability and Mercerian theorems.

  • Tuesday 27th November 2007

    Mike Evans (Washington and Lee) : Relationships among the following types of integration: Riemann, Lebesgue, Henstock-Kurzweil and First-Return.

  • Tuesday 6th November 2007

    Lars Olsen: Distribution of digits in integers: Besicovitch-Eggleston subsets of N, abstract .

  • Tuesday 30th October 2007

    Tony Samuel: Convergence of the Ruelle Operator for a function satisfying Bowen's Condition.

  • Tuesday 23rd October 2007

    Kenneth Falconer: Aspects of self-affine sets.

  • Tuesday 16th October 2007

    Sara Munday: Minkowski's question mark function.

  • Tuesday 9th October 2007

    Kenneth Falconer: Representing families of sets by measures and multifractal analysis.
