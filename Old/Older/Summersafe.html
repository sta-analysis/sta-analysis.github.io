Analysis Research Group

Every Year within the Analysis Group an eight week summer internship is offered to 3 or 4 students intending to pursue a Ph.D within Mathematics. The Summer School is run by Prof. K. Falconer and Dr. Lars Olsen. Students interested in an internship for the Summer 2008 should contact Dr. Lars Olsen.

The reports of some of the recent Summer School students are advailable as pdf files.
