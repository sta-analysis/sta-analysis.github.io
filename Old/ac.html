Additive Combinatorics Reading Group Spring 2017

This reading group aims to provide an introduction to additive combinatorics starting from the basics and is open to all. The group meets Wednesdays 15:00 in Room 3B and each talk is given by a member of the group but everyone is encouraged to participate and discuss the material. Currently we are working through Tuomas Orponen's lecture notes. For any questions contact Han Yu at hy25 at st dash andrews dot ac.uk.
