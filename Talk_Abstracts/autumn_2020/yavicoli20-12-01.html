I will present a result that shows that any Lebesgue-measurable set in ℝd with density larger than (n-2)/(n-1) contains similar copies of every n-point set at all sufficiently large scales. I will also give an example to show that the density required to guarantee all large similar copies of n-point sets tends to 1 at a rate 1- O(n-1/5logn). Joint work with Kenneth Falconer and Vjekoslav Kovač.
