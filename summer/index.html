Summer School Projects

Every summer we arrange internships lasting around 6 weeks for a number of undergraduates, both from St Andrews and other universities, who are thinking about doing a PhD in mathematics. Interested students should contact Dr Richard Scott. There are various funding opportunities available, including:

  • The Laidlaw Leadership and Research Programme
  • Carnegie Trust Vacation Scholarships
  • LMS Undergraduate Research Bursaries
